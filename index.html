Census Field Mapper

India Census field survey tool. Tracks your path and lets you mark houses as Done, Skipped, or Revisit.

Location permission tip:
  1. When you tap Start Tracking inside the app, your browser will ask for location access — tap Allow
  2. If it doesn't ask, tap the lock 🔒 in the address bar → Location → Allow → Reload
  3. Use Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone) for best GPS support
